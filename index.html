The problem

Most CEOs are already overwhelmed before they consider AI.

You don't need more recommendations. You need actual infrastructure — a system that holds your context so you don't have to re-brief it every conversation.

  • No single source of truth for decisions, relationships, projects.
  • Meeting prep and follow-up consume hours per week with no system behind them.
  • Context-switching between ventures costs 20–40 minutes of re-orientation each time.
  • Important information lives across email, Slack, documents and memory — none of it connected.
  • AI tools have been tried and abandoned because they lack personal context.

The CEO AI OS doesn't add another tool. It adds a layer of intelligence around the tools you already use.

How it works

Three phases. One outcome: a working system.

  1. 01

    Discovery session

    60 minutes · Recorded · No prep required

    A structured conversation across five sections — your world, your time, your stack, your ideal state, your priorities. The session itself surfaces every pain point worth solving. One hour is the appropriate ask of a CEO.

  2. 02

    Vault build

    Within 1 week · Delivered to you

    The transcript becomes a vault: a navigation guide, context files, a brief per venture, a profile per key contact, your tool stack connected via MCP, and the top 2–3 modules activated and running from day one.

  3. 03

    30-day check-in

    45 minutes · Real usage data

    A structured review of what's working, what's not, and what the next 60 days look like. Module adjustments based on the way you actually used the system — not the way we assumed you would.

The eight modules

Configurable. Activated by priority — not all at once.

Your Q25 ranking in the discovery session decides what goes first. The rest waits until the first one is stable.

M1 Morning Intelligence Brief

Delivered before your inbox opens. Day summary, decisions needed, email priorities, per-meeting context.

M2 Meeting-to-Action Pipeline

Transcript → structured summary → action items → draft follow-up. You review and approve. Nothing sends without you.

M3 Living Context Memory

Always-current briefs for every project and venture. Context-switching takes 90 seconds, not 20 minutes.

M4 Stakeholder Intelligence

People profiles, relationship history, last touchpoints, open commitments. Meeting prep on demand.

M5 Universal Retrieval

Searches email, notes, documents, decisions. Answers "what did we decide about X?" in seconds.

M6 Deep Work Architecture

Defends strategic thinking time. Manages inbox during focus blocks. Structural permission to be unreachable.

M7 Weekly Review & Planning

End-of-week summary plus start-of-week priorities. What happened, what's outstanding, what matters most.

M8 Research & Intelligence Briefs

Scheduled briefings on competitor moves, industry signals and topics relevant to your context.

Who it's for

Aware but stuck. Tech-curious. Time-poor.

Right fit

  • CEO, MD or Founder — Australian, $5M–$100M turnover (or ambitious pre-revenue).
  • Running multiple ventures, boards or advisory roles.
  • Has tried AI tools and found them generic.
  • Aware the problem is structural, not just workload.
  • Respects doing over advising — wants a working system, not a consultation.

Not the right fit

  • You have a high-performing EA already managing your context and your week.
  • You're privacy-averse to the point of blocking any cloud-based integration.
  • You want a quick fix — the system requires a real conversation and consistent use.

Trust & portability

Built on open standards. Yours to keep, regardless of provider.

Three security tiers

Standard API for most clients. Zero Data Retention for ASX-listed, professional services and legal — at no extra cost. Air-gapped local LLM (Ollama) for government and highly regulated industries.

Markdown throughout

Your vault is plain markdown — the most portable, future-proof format available. Not locked to any LLM provider. Open it in any editor, on any machine.

Open MCP standard

Model Context Protocol is now a Linux Foundation standard, co-backed by Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Microsoft and AWS. Switch providers any time — your context moves with you.

Low ongoing cost

Typical API spend: $3–8/month for daily personal use. Free Gemini tier available for clients who want zero billing friction. The system runs on infrastructure you control.
