Automations that work

Hours back. Every single day.

Eight automations, configured to your priorities. Activated by what would save you the most time first — never all at once.

~30 min / day

Morning Intelligence Brief

Lands before your inbox opens. Day summary, decisions needed, email priorities, per-meeting context. You walk into the day already briefed.

~15 min / meeting

Meeting-to-Action Pipeline

Transcript becomes a structured summary, decision log, action items, and a draft follow-up email. You review, approve, send.

~18 min / switch

Living Context Memory

Always-current briefs for every project and venture. Read in two minutes. Walk into the next call with full context.

~20 min / meeting

Stakeholder Intelligence

People profiles, relationship history, last touchpoints, open commitments. Meeting prep on demand, in seconds.

~10 min / search

Universal Retrieval

Searches email, notes, documents, decisions. "What did we decide about X?" — answered in seconds, with the source.

~90 min / day

Deep Work Architecture

~30 min / week

Weekly Review & Planning

End-of-week summary. Start-of-week priorities. What happened, what's outstanding, what matters most this week.

~1 hr / week

Research & Intelligence Briefs

Scheduled briefings on competitor moves, industry signals and topics that matter to your context. Read on a Sunday, ready for Monday.

Who it's for

Aware but stuck. Tech-curious. Time-poor.

Right fit

  • CEO, MD or Founder — Australian, $5M–$100M turnover (or ambitious pre-revenue).
  • Running multiple ventures, boards or advisory roles.
  • Has tried AI tools and found them generic.
  • Aware the problem is structural, not just workload.
  • Respects doing over advising — wants a working system, not a consultation.

Not the right fit

  • You have a high-performing EA already managing your context and your week.
  • You're privacy-averse to the point of blocking any cloud-based integration.
  • You want a quick fix — the system requires a real conversation and consistent use.

Trust & portability

Built on open standards. Yours to keep, regardless of provider.

Three security tiers

Standard API for most clients. Zero Data Retention for ASX-listed, professional services and legal — at no extra cost. Air-gapped local LLM for government and highly regulated industries.

Markdown throughout

Your vault is plain markdown — the most portable, future-proof format available. Not locked to any LLM provider. Open it in any editor, on any machine.

Open MCP standard

Model Context Protocol is now a Linux Foundation standard, co-backed by Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Microsoft and AWS. Switch providers any time — your context moves with you.

Low ongoing cost

Typical API spend: $3–8/month for daily personal use. Free Gemini tier available for clients who want zero billing friction. The system runs on infrastructure you control.
